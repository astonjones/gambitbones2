FAQ

Common Questions

Still have questions? Use the contact form below and we'll get back to you.

Is there a cost to have a machine placed at my business?

No — there is no upfront cost to businesses. We handle machine placement, installation, stocking, and maintenance at no charge to you. We earn revenue from product sales, making it a win-win for your business.

What types of products can be stocked in the machine?

We offer a wide variety of products including snacks, candy, cold beverages, protein bars and shakes, fresh meals, and specialty items. Every machine is tailored to the preferences and needs of your specific location.

How quickly can a machine be installed?

After we assess your space and finalize the placement agreement, most installations are completed within a few business days. We handle all logistics — you just pick the spot.

What payment methods do your machines accept?

Our machines accept all major credit and debit cards, contactless payments (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash where applicable. We're cashless-first because it's faster, cleaner, and more convenient.

How are machines monitored and restocked?

Our machines are connected to an AI-powered monitoring system that tracks inventory levels in real time. We receive automated restocking alerts and schedule service visits proactively — so your machine is never empty or running stale product.

What areas do you currently serve?

We serve Southeast Texas — including Beaumont, Port Arthur, Orange, Nederland, Lumberton, Vidor, and surrounding communities — as well as the greater Houston metro area. Contact us to confirm availability at your specific location.
